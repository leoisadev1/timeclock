Business rules:
- Employees can only see published schedules.
- Open shifts are visible after publish but read-only for employees.
- Publishing is a visibility toggle and records publishedAt/updatedAt.
- Warnings do not block publishing.
- One open timecard per employee at a time.
- Multiple closed timecards per employee per day are allowed.
- Clock-ins are allowed without a matching shift.
- Matching rule: same employee, same location, published schedule, shift starts on same business date, clock-in within 2 hours before/after scheduled start, nearest start wins.
- Attendance status: early >5 min before, on time from 5 min before through 5 min after, late >5 min after, no-show if no clock-in 15 min after scheduled start, unscheduled when no matched shift.
- Open shifts do not auto-assign on clock-in.
- Corrections require a note and preserve before/after in timecardEdits.
- Normal UI should not delete historical timecards or edit records.
